The Beauty and the Beast

Gabrielle-Suzanne Barbot de Villeneuve

To be honest here, mainly bought this book because it was illustrated by Minalima and had interactive elements. Additionally, it was my favourite Disney movie. I know, I know, Disney makes classic novels such as Beauty and the Beast and Cinderella kid friendly, so parts of the story are changed. Saying that, did not need to know that beauty and the beast are cousins, that was a shock for me to say the least. Nonetheless, it was fun to read parts of story that were not included in the Disney movie.
