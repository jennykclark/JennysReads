The Cleaner

Mark Dawson

I had higher hopes for this novel. I was not a fan of the start of the novel. Once again, I kept reading because I wanted to see how the novel would end. The tension in the book kept getting higher and higher and more interesting then at the very end, it went downhill. I might need to reread the novel to fully understand why I felt the ending was not appropriate for the novel.
