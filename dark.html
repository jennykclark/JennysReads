My Dark Vanessa

Kate Elizabeth Russell

The way Russell deals with the dark and difficult topic, results in an eye opener read and a novel everyone should read. It takes you through the years of a young female student who has experience manipulative and destructive by the hands of an older male teacher, which she believes is love. The extremely hard topic was written in a powerful way. It is a powerful piece of work, but at the same time is also unsettling.
