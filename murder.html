The Thursday Murder Club

Richard Osman

The concept of the book, is great, the reason I bought the book. Nevertheless, I disliked the book. The start of the book was rocky, I was unsure how the book was a murder mystery book, but I kept on reading. I found it a dull read, I lost track of the characters and how some characters that were introduced had relationships with another. In my opinion, the story idea was good, a murder mystery set in a retirement home, but the execution was not the best.

You are able to buy this book : Waterstones.
