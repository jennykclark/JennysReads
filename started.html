He Started It

Samantha Downing

This was an interesting read. I thought this book would just be about siblings on a road trip to scatter their grandpa’s ashes but is it so much more. When you continue to read, you start to realise there are dark secrets that are about to be uncovered. Each sibling is a dick each in their own ways, this makes the novel feel more real, like something you watch on a Netflix documentary. Each page is filled with its own suspensive, you think one thing is going to happen but then does not. Then your shocked at the outcome of the characters’ actions. The ending, that ending, after reading it I just sat there because I was shocked, I did not think it would happen but then you start to realise it all makes sense.
